 The Keil RT-Agent is deprecated in MDK-ARM 4.60
=================================================

The RT-Agent component of Keil MDK-ARM is no longer
actively maintained. The component is shipped "as is"
an no updates or bug fixes will be provided.

The files RT_Agent.c and RT_Agent.h in this folder are included
for compatibility in this release but will be removed
from the product in future releases.
